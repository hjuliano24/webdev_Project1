Cats

I love cat's. This page talks about cats I find interesting.

Tuxedo cat (not breed but for fur pattern)

-their bellies, chests and paws are white while the rest of their body is black, orange or gray

Calico Cats (not breed but fur pattern)

-three color coat combination (white, black and red orange)

-Depending on the culture can represent money(US) or good luck(Japan)

-Japanese sailors used to bring them to their voyage for good luck.

-Most are female(genetics is related to the x chromosome)
